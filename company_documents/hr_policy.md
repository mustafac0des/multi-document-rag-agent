# HR Policies and Hiring Guide

## 1. Hiring Philosophy

Our hiring philosophy is the foundation of our company culture. We prioritize extreme talent density, seeking out only the most capable individuals in their respective fields. We strictly evaluate for cultural alignment, ensuring that new hires embody our core values. Above all, we look for an ownership mindset, where candidates demonstrate a history of taking profound responsibility for their work.

## 2. Hiring Process

We employ a rigorous and structured four step hiring process. First, we conduct a thorough resume screening to identify candidates who meet our baseline qualifications. Second, candidates undergo a demanding technical interview to validate their hard skills. Third, we hold a culture fit interview to assess their alignment with our values. Finally, all potential hires must pass a final executive review before an offer is extended.

## 3. Compensation Guidelines

Our compensation packages are designed to attract and retain elite talent. We consistently offer salaries within established, competitive salary bands. We dynamically adjust these offers based on the candidate's specific experience and market value. Furthermore, we systematically include significant equity grants for all senior roles to align long term incentives.

## 4. Performance Management

Performance management at Aether Corp is an ongoing dialogue. We conduct formal quarterly check ins to ensure alignment on goals and provide timely feedback. These culminate in comprehensive annual reviews that determine overall performance ratings. Based on these reviews, we hold structured promotion cycles to recognize and reward exceptional contributions.

## 5. Employee Retention

We invest heavily in retaining our top performers. We maintain highly competitive salaries that reflect the current market conditions. We actively provide structured growth opportunities and clear paths for career advancement. Furthermore, we believe that transparent leadership and open communication are critical for maintaining high employee morale and loyalty.

## 6. Conflict Resolution

We aim to resolve interpersonal conflicts swiftly and professionally. Our first line of resolution involves structured mediation facilitated by the Human Resources department. If a conflict cannot be resolved at this level, we have a clear process for escalation to senior leadership for a final, binding decision.

## 7. Termination Policy

Employment termination is handled with clarity and decisiveness. We maintain a clear policy for performance based termination, which occurs after a documented period of inadequate improvement. Conversely, we execute immediate termination for severe violations of our code of conduct or security policies.

## 8. Diversity and Inclusion

We actively aim to build a diverse and inclusive workplace across all teams. We believe that a variety of perspectives and backgrounds is essential for driving innovation and making robust business decisions. We are committed to fostering an environment where everyone feels welcomed and empowered to contribute.
